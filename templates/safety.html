Few Tips to keep you Safe.
  • Never click on any suspecious URL that you may have recieved through any mail or message.
  • Always check the URL/domain before clicking it.
  • If opened, never give any details to any suspecious website. Example: If the domain has a random string and the page asks for some credentials, never submit those.
  • For more, read here: https://consumer.ftc.gov/articles/how-recognize-and-avoid-phishing-scams
